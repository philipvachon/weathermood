WeatherMood

Make a Spotify playlist with the mood of any city's weather.

    Using Spotify and Echonest's API, along with OpenWeather API, I am able to convert the weather of a city into a specific 'mood' of songs to reflect that weather. The 'Energy' of a song is represented by wind speed, and the 'Valence' (how positive or negative the sentiment of the song is) is represented by cloud cover. Those two data points create the mood of the playlist, which you can see in this graph. I also used components from Materialize CSS and Roadtrip Playlist by Echonest's Paul Lamere.
